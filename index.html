About Us

Warner Bros., established in 1923 by the Warner brothers, is a prominent entertainment company known for its extensive contributions to film and television production. It has produced numerous blockbuster movies, TV series, and video games, featuring iconic franchises such as Harry Potter, DC Comics adaptations, and The Lord of the Rings. The company also owns television production divisions responsible for creating hit shows like Friends and Game of Thrones. Over the years, Warner Bros. has diversified its portfolio into distribution, streaming services, and theme parks, cementing its significant impact on global popular culture.
